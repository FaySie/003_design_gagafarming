About Da-guan tribe

雪山山脈上最熱情友善的村落

The most warm and friendly villages in the snowy Mountains.

位於臺中市和平區西北端， 大安溪上游之泰雅原住民部落。
部落名稱由來：L'olu是一種像茅草的植物名字， 因當初達觀部落的土地非常潮濕且長滿了莖像細水管的L'olu， 所以達觀部落以此植物名稱命名。
Located in the northwest end of Heping District, Taichung City, a Tayal Aboriginal tribe lie in upstream of Da-an River.
Origin of tribe's name: L'olu is a name of a thatch-like plant. The tribe is named after the plant as the lands of the tribe were wet and covered by L'olu.

GaGa傳統 / GaGa Tradition

泰雅部落有「共食共作」的Gaga傳統， 是祖先的遺訓更是部落共同的規範準則。 部落一起耕作、打獵、收穫、分享， 親族之間一起共食共享， 是一個很基本、支持性也很強的傳統照顧體系。
Tayal tribe has a “gaga” tradition, which means “eating and working together”, the whole tribe farming, hunting, harvesting and sharing together. Between relatives, they share all the food they have together. For them, the idea of sharing, being supportive is very basic caring system in the tribe.

部落共同廚房誕生 / Tribal Common Kitchen

延伸泰雅Gaga祖先的遺訓， 達觀部落的「部落共同廚房」就此誕生， 其開發泰雅美食、特色咖啡、小米酒、 傳統編織工藝、特色烘焙、 深度旅遊等產品成為營業場所。
Extended by the teachings from the ancestors of Ta-yal Gaga, Da-guan tribe's "Tribal common kitchen" was born. It develops Ta-yal cuisine, specialty coffee, rice wine, traditional weaving, specialty baking, tourism and other products to become a business premise. Their main industry is sweet persimmon; the tribal kitchen is also a local Feature of Da-guan tribe.

達觀部落 Da-guan Tribe

在台中和苗栗分界的大安溪畔， 散落著13個泰雅族部落。
我們在此成立了部落廚房－深耕德瑪汶協會， 用部落自己的力量開發泰雅特色產業－工藝品、特色餐點、深度旅遊， 以所得回饋給部落， 照顧當地的弱勢族群－老人、學童、婦女， 一邊培養部落的青年、進行學童的課輔， 還要向您介紹泰雅的文化！
